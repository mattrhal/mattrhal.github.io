One Services Staffing, LLC, is a premier commercial janitorial and hospitality staffing provider based in Dallas, Texas. Established by industry veteran Pam Haley, the company is a certified Women Business Enterprise (WBE) and Small Business Enterprise (SBE) with a strong reputation for excellence in specialized facility maintenance.

Core Business Offerings

The company operates through two integrated divisions: - Commercial Janitorial Services: Comprehensive cleaning solutions including nightly maintenance, specialized floor care (stripping, waxing, and carpet extraction), and hot-water pressure washing for high-grease areas. They also provide post-construction and commercial kitchen deep cleaning. - Hospitality & Staffing: Specialized staffing for high-end hotels and resorts, including housekeepers, laundry attendants, food and beverage servers, banquet staff, and prep cooks.

Target Customers

One Services Staffing primarily serves high-end commercial clients in the Dallas-Fort Worth area and beyond: - Luxury Hospitality: Hotels and resorts requiring meticulous cleaning and flexible staffing. - Commercial Real Estate: Property management firms and multi-tenant office buildings. - Leisure & Retail: Restaurants, shopping centers, and fitness facilities. - Industrial & Construction: Developers needing final clean-up services after major renovations.

Company Vision and Culture

  • Vibe: Professional, reliable, and results-oriented. The company emphasizes a “partnership” model rather than a simple vendor-client relationship.
  • Goals: To redefine service excellence by exceeding standard service level agreements (SLAs) through rigorous quality control.
  • Execution: They employ a dedicated Director of Quality Assurance to conduct routine inspections and performance checks, ensuring consistency across all service locations.

Unique Attributes and Practices

  • Diverse Ownership & Partnerships: It is a pioneer woman-owned janitorial firm in the region and a Certified Chickasaw Nation Vendor, highlighting its commitment to diversity.
  • Specialized Expertise: Leadership has over 35 years of hands-on experience in specialized floor care and hotel operations, providing a level of domain expertise that general staffing agencies often lack.
  • Regulatory Compliance: The firm is OSHA Certified and an active member of the International Facility Management Association (IFMA) and the Hotel & Lodging Association (HLA).
